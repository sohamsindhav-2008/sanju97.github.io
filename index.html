From Ignorance to Elegance

Yesterday's Man of the match Sanju samson who came to open the innings and played the sensational knock of 97(50)*.The beauty of that inning was that it was completely risk free and his shot selection was top notch. He has different stance for spinners and pacer , he completed his fifty with sr of 203 but he was not just blindly slogging he was respecting the good deliveries and finding gaps and punishing the off ones, he was holding the one end and led India to the semis.
